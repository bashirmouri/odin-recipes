Tagine

Description

A traditional Moroccan dish, served with bread that is incredibly tasty and nice to eat.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Wash and cut vegetables
  2. Add spices to meat
  3. Put meat and vegetables on pan and cook
  4. Add water and let it cook till there is a little left
